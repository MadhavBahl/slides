What to do if you made a mistake?

Being Better Series Episode 7

Remember, everyone makes mistakes, and that's how people learn

Making mistakes is fine, but

Make sure you don't

1. Hide your mistakes

2. Be Defensive

3. Blame Others

Steps to recover from the mistake

1. Communicate Early

Even if it's a bad news

2. Admit Quickly

Don't delay

3. Explain how it happened

And have some mitigation steps in mind

4. Learn from it

And don't let it happen again

5. Let the loss recover with time
